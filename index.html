About

Hi, I’m Yassine — a passionate Video Editor and Motion Graphics Designer with 6 years of experience bringing ideas to life through visual storytelling. I specialize in crafting engaging videos, dynamic animations, and clean typography that communicate powerful messages. Over the years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects ranging from promotional content to brand visuals, gaining a deep understanding of how motion and design can capture attention and elevate a brand’s identity
